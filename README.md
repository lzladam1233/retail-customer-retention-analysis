# Retail Customer Retention & Churn Risk Analysis

## Business Problem
A retail membership program observed declining repeat purchase behavior. 
Goal: Identify drivers of churn and revenue differences across tiers.

## Dataset
- 1M+ transactional records
- 3 membership tiers (Bronze, Silver, Gold)
- Customer satisfaction ratings
- Purchase frequency metrics

## Key Insights
- Gold members generate 2.4x revenue vs Bronze
- Customers rating 3.3–4.1 show 20% slower repurchase velocity
- 46% churn risk concentrated in mid-satisfaction segment

## Tools Used
- SQL (ETL & aggregation)
- Python (EDA)
- BI Dashboard (Power BI / Qlik)
- KPI modeling

## Business Recommendation
- Target 3.3–4.1 rating customers with retention incentives
- Upsell Silver to Gold based on satisfaction patterns
